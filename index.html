Looking For Refrigeration?

Dry Freight Containers

All Seabox Depot containers are available to be seen, felt and touched prior to purchase. All of our container sizes can be adjusted in length, width and height to allow for greater versatility. Each unit, new or used ensures the utmost protection of your assets with assured water, wind and light tight features. As a standard, all basic dry freight containers come equipped with a pair of lockable double doors on one end, marine grade plywood flooring and rugged corrugated Corten steel paneling.

Modified Units

Seabox Depot understands unique requirements and knows that modifications to a container can be necessary to achieve structure goals. By sharing our design expertise, we ensure our clients walk away knowing they made an educated purchase.

Products & Parts

Replacement parts and container accessories are a fundamental component to the productivity of companies working in the freight industry. Wear and tear are an unavoidable part of use over time. As a proud distributor of an extensive inventory of parts in Alberta, our replacement parts and accessories are designed to withstand the test of time.

Rental Solutions

Helping our customers find the perfect space management solution when a permanent one isn’t required is a huge component of our business. With an ever-growing rental fleet, Seabox is ready and willing to accommodate your needs. We offer a variety of sizes, colours and multipurpose modular units to serve your short-term storage and budget needs..
